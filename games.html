SIMON'S GAME

Steps to Play Simon:

Step 1 : Initiate the Game: Look for the "Start" button and press it to begin the game.

Step 2 : Observe Simon's Pattern: Simon will start by lighting up one of the colored buttons accompanied by a sound. Watch and listen attentively to remember the sequence.

Step 3 : Repeat Simon's Pattern: Once Simon finishes playing the initial sequence, it's your turn. Replicate the same sequence by pressing the buttons in the exact order that Simon displayed. If Simon lit up the red, blue, green buttons in that order, you must press those same buttons in the same sequence

Step 4 : Correct Sequence: If you successfully repeat the pattern, Simon will add another step to the sequence, making it longer. Watch the new sequence carefully and replicate it again.

Step 5 : Continue and Progress: Keep repeating Simon's sequences correctly. With each successful repetition, Simon will add one more step, making the sequence longer and more challenging.

Step 6 : Remember and Repeat: Concentrate on remembering the growing sequence and pressing the buttons accurately in the same order.

Step 7 : End of Game: The game ends when you make a mistake by pressing the wrong button or pressing them in the wrong order. At this point, Simon will usually produce a sound or light indicating an incorrect move.

Step 8 : Scorekeeping: Simon keeps track of your score, which is the number of steps (button presses) you accurately repeated in a row. Try to beat your previous high score in subsequent games.

Step 9 : Restart or Play Again: Once the game ends, you can start a new round by pressing the "Retart" button to try to surpass your previous score.
